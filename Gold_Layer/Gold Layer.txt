3. Gold Layer: Business Aggregates (The "Knowledge" Layer)
This layer is optimized for reporting and analytics. It contains highly joined tables or specific aggregates. We can use dbt to manage these transformations, as it excels at documenting business logic and running data quality tests.

Snowflake Feature: dbt (for modeling) & Dynamic Tables (for real-time dashboards).

Action: Create a "Fact" table that summarizes EV adoption by region or manufacturer.

CREATE OR REPLACE DYNAMIC TABLE EV_RESEARCH.PUBLIC.GOLD_EV_STATE_METRICS
  TARGET_LAG = '1 hour'
  WAREHOUSE = COMPUTE_WH
AS
SELECT
    state,
    model_year,
    COUNT(vin) AS total_vehicles,
    COUNT(DISTINCT make) AS unique_makes,
    COUNT(DISTINCT model) AS unique_models,
    COUNT_IF(ev_type = 'Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV)') AS bev_count,
    COUNT_IF(ev_type = 'Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV)') AS phev_count,
    ROUND(COUNT_IF(ev_type = 'Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV)') / NULLIF(COUNT(*), 0) * 100, 2) AS bev_pct,
    ROUND(COUNT_IF(ev_type = 'Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV)') / NULLIF(COUNT(*), 0) * 100, 2) AS phev_pct
FROM
    EV_RESEARCH.PUBLIC.SILVER_EV_CLEANED
GROUP BY state, model_year

Analyze BEV adoption trends over model years in GOLD_EV_STATE_METRICS to identify if newer vehicles skew more electric
-- BEV Adoption Trend by Model Year
SELECT
    model_year,
    SUM(total_vehicles) AS total_vehicles,
    SUM(bev_count) AS total_bev,
    SUM(phev_count) AS total_phev,
    ROUND(SUM(bev_count) / NULLIF(SUM(total_vehicles), 0) * 100, 2) AS bev_pct,

ORDER BY model_year;

Yes, newer vehicles strongly skew toward BEV. Clear upward trend:

Period	BEV %	Trend

2015–2017	56–74%	BEV gaining ground
2018–2020	69–84%	BEV pulling ahead significantly
2021–2022	81–85%	BEV dominant

Key findings:

BEV adoption climbed from ~49% (2012) to ~93% (2023) — a 44-point increase
PHEV share dropped from 51% (2012) to just 7% (2023)
The inflection point was 2018–2019, when BEV jumped from 69% to 81%, likely driven by Tesla Model 3 availability
2022 had the highest volume (4,997 vehicles) at 85% BEV
2023 is partial-year data (1,356 vehicles) but shows the strongest BEV skew yet

Identify which vehicle makes and models are driving the BEV adoption trend in recent years (2022-2023)

-- Top Models Driving BEV Adoption (2022-2023)
SELECT
    make,
    model,
    model_year,
    COUNT(*) AS total,
    ROUND(COUNT(*) / SUM(COUNT(*)) OVER () * 100, 2) AS pct_of_all_bev
FROM EV_RESEARCH.PUBLIC.SILVER_EV_CLEANED
WHERE model_year IN (2022, 2023)
  AND ev_type = 'Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV)'
GROUP BY make, model, model_year
ORDER BY total DESC
LIMIT 20;
